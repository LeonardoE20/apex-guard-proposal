01 · The Vision

A SMARTER WAY
TO STAFF SECURITY

Josue's platform fills a real gap — no reliable app exists that lets an independent security company post shifts, reward guard loyalty, and sell gear all in one place.

🏢
The Business Model
Josue contracts with companies needing security — Allied, event venues, construction sites, temp contracts. Those shifts get posted on the platform and guards claim them.
🛡️
The Guard Experience
Guards sign up, pick up shifts on their schedule, and earn rank as they build a track record. Higher rank means higher hourly pay. No leaderboard — just personal growth.
💰
How Revenue Works
A company pays $55/hr. Josue pays the guard their rank rate ($21–$32). The margin is his business income — and guards are motivated to stay loyal to earn more.
🏪
Uniform Store
Guards buy uniforms in-app — shirts, pants, patches, badges. An additional revenue stream that keeps guards looking sharp and on-brand at every site.
02 · How It Works

FROM CONTRACT
TO PAYCHECK

A clear flow from company contract to guard payout. Josue manages both ends — companies never need app access.

01
Josue signs a contract
He negotiates with a company — Allied, a construction site, an event venue — and agrees on a rate (e.g., $55/hr for a guard).
02
Shift gets posted on the app
Josue creates the shift in the admin panel: company, location, date, time, spots needed. It appears immediately on the guard shift board.
03
Guards claim the shift
Eligible guards see the shift and tap to claim it. They see their personal pay rate based on their rank — not the full company rate.
04
Guard shows up, shift completed
After the shift, admin marks it complete. The guard's shift count increases and the rank engine checks if they've earned a promotion.
05
Guard gets paid
Payout is logged in the system. Josue keeps the margin. The guard sees their earnings clearly in the app's payout dashboard.
